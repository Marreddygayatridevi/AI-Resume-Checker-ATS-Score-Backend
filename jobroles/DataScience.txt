Role: Data Scientist

Summary:
A Data Scientist utilizes advanced analytics, machine learning, and statistical methods to extract insights from large and complex datasets. They build predictive models and provide data-driven solutions to business challenges.

Key Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and preprocess large datasets from multiple sources
- Develop and implement machine learning models and algorithms
- Perform exploratory data analysis and feature engineering
- Interpret and communicate findings to technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deploy data-driven solutions
- Stay updated with the latest data science trends and technologies

Required Skills:
- Proficiency in Python or R for data analysis and modeling
- Experience with machine learning frameworks (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Strong knowledge of statistics and probability
- Familiarity with SQL and data manipulation tools
- Data visualization skills (e.g., Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau)
- Excellent problem-solving and critical thinking abilities

Education:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Data Science, or a related field

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Previous experience in a data science or related role
- Understanding of deep learning and natural language processing
