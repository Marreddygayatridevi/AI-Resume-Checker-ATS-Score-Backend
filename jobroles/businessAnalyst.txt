Job Role: Data Analyst

Summary:
A Data Analyst collects, processes, and performs statistical analyses on large datasets. They discover how data can be used to answer questions and solve problems.

Key Responsibilities:
- Collect and interpret data from various sources.
- Analyze results using statistical techniques.
- Develop and implement data analyses, data collection systems, and other strategies.
- Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.
- Prepare reports and present findings to stakeholders.

Required Skills:
- Proficiency in SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Experience with statistical programming languages (e.g., Python, R).
- Attention to detail and accuracy.
- Good communication and presentation skills.
